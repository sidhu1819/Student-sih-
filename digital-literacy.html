Welcome to Computer Basics!

Let's learn about the main parts of a computer. Knowing these parts helps you use the computer better for learning and fun!

What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic machine that processes information. You can use it to write, draw, play games, find information, and much more.

The Monitor (Screen)

The monitor is like a television screen. It shows you everything you are working on, like text, pictures, and videos.

The Keyboard

The keyboard has many buttons called 'keys'. You press these keys to type letters, numbers, and symbols onto the screen.

The Mouse

The mouse is a small device you move with your hand. It moves a pointer (or cursor) on the screen to click on things and open programs.

Staying Safe Online

The internet is a wonderful place to learn and have fun, but it's important to be safe. Here are three golden rules to always remember!

  • Keep Your Information Private. Never share your full name, home address, school name, or phone number with strangers online.
  • Be Kind and Respectful. Treat others online the same way you would in real life. If you see someone being mean, don't join in.
  • Tell a Trusted Adult. If anything online makes you feel sad, scared, or uncomfortable, tell a parent, guardian, or teacher right away.
